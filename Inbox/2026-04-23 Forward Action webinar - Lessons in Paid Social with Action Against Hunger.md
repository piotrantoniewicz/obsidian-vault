Lessons in Paid Social: Building an Always-On Digital Growth Programme | Webinar Recording  
  
How do you build a paid social programme that consistently recruits new supporters and drives online donations?  
  
In this webinar recording, Forward Action and Action Against Hunger share how they built a high-performing, always-on paid social strategy from the ground up - generating nearly 50,000 new supporters in 2025 and raising over £500,000 in online donations.  
  
Join Beth Miles (Forward Action) and Jimmy Perkins (Action Against Hunger) as they break down the strategy, creative, and testing approach behind one of the sector’s most effective digital fundraising programmes.  
  
What you’ll learn:  
  
\- How to recruit supporters at scale using paid social  
\- Why lo-fi, reactive creative is outperforming traditional ads  
\- How to respond to the news cycle for better campaign performance  
\- The role of video in paid social campaigns in 2026  
\- What’s working (and not working) in direct-to-donate campaigns  
\- How to build a test-and-learn culture that improves results  
  
Who this is for:  
  
\- Charity professionals working in digital, fundraising, or comms  
\- Teams looking to grow their supporter base or increase online donations  
\- Anyone running (or planning) paid social campaigns
