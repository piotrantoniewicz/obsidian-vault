Donald Trump’s second administration has wasted no time in aggressively collapsing the government, eroding democratic norms, and targeting institutions that provide checks on his power. In just a few weeks, his administration has moved to freeze foreign aid, purged oversight officials across federal agencies, laid off thousands of government workers, and initiated a sweeping review of international treaties. He has expanded executive authority at an unprecedented pace, sidelining Congress and the courts while aligning his administration with corporate elites like Elon Musk to dismantle public institutions. From gutting the federal workforce to reshaping trade policy, Trump’s actions are not just a continuation of his first term—they represent a fundamental dismantling of American governance with little precedent.

The threats to workers, immigrants, marginalized communities, and democratic institutions demand urgent action. While we’re focused on building long-term power and capacity through the [**Higher Ground Institute**](https://highergroundlabs.com/institute/), we must also respond in real time to resist Trump’s agenda and defend democracy. Across the country, grassroots movements, advocacy organizations, and progressive campaigns are mobilizing. Technology is playing a critical role in these efforts, equipping activists with better ways to coordinate, track legislative threats, and amplify their message with precision.

Here are some of the tools available right now to support our fight:
