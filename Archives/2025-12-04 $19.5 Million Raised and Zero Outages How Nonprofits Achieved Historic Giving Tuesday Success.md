## 2025 Report Card: Historic Fundraising Results

  
The collective hard work of our clients resulted in record-breaking generosity on the Engaging Networks platform, demonstrating significant growth across the board:

- **$19.5 million** was raised for mission-driven organizations across the platform (in U.S. dollars).
- This total represents a **10% increase** in funds raised compared to Giving Tuesday 2024.
- Overall, giving volume soared, with over **182,000 gifts** processed, marking an **8% increase** in the number of gifts.

Perhaps most encouragingly, the average gift amount saw an increase of **3.6% YOY**, settling at **$116**. This suggests that strong campaigns combined with a seamless user experience can cultivate greater value from every donor.

## The Foundation: Scale Without the Stress

When it comes to technology on Giving Tuesday, the ultimate metric isn’t just how much traffic you handle—it’s whether the platform runs flawlessly.

We are proud to report that, thanks to careful preparation and continuous refinement, **Engaging Networks clients experienced zero platform outages.**

This reliability is crucial because half of the day’s fundraising — **$9.9 Million** — was processed during the peak hours between 3 PM and 11 PM ET. When the moment mattered most, the platform was rock-solid.
