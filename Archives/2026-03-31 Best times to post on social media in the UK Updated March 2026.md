## Best times to post on social media in the UK

**Quick Answer:** The overall best times to post on social media in the UK are **Thursday afternoons and evenings**, specifically between 4 – 11 p.m. GMT.

- **Best day to post in the UK:** Thursday
- **Worst day to post in the UK:** Saturday

Averaged across all platforms, engagement data reveals a distinct, sustained pattern in how the UK interacts with social media. Monitoring these macro trends contextualizes your content calendar against the wider digital landscape:

| Behavioral Trend | Peak Timeframe | Audience Insight |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **The afternoon-to-evening shift** | 4 p.m. onwards | Traditional daytime peaks have softened, making way for a longer, sustained stretch of engagement that kicks off around 4 p.m. and lasts through the evening. |
| **The hybrid work effect** | Post-workday transition | Remote and hybrid work models have blurred the lines between standard working hours and downtime, meaning UK audiences are catching up on feeds as they transition out of the workday. |
| **Thursday peaks & weekend unplugs** | Thursday vs. Saturday | Thursday provides the widest and most active window of the week. Conversely, engagement naturally drops by Saturday as people actively unplug for the weekend. |

## Best time to post on social media in the UK - FAQ

What is the overall best time to post on social media in the UK?

**The overall best time to post on social media in the UK is Thursday between 4:00 p.m. and 11:00 p.m.** Based on our 2026 data, user engagement across all major networks consistently peaks during this sustained late-afternoon and evening window as professionals begin their digital decompression.

**Yes, time zones matter. S** **chedule your content based on the local time zone of your target audience.** If your primary audience is in the UK, ensure your publishing schedule is strictly set to GMT (or BST during the summer).

What is the worst day to post on social media in the UK?

**Saturday is the worst day to post on social media in the UK.** Across networks like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and TikTok, engagement drops to its lowest point on Saturdays as users step away from their screens to enjoy their weekend downtime.

Do social media algorithms care about what time you post?

**Yes, recency is an important ranking signal for almost all social media algorithms.** Posting exactly when your audience is actively online ensures your content receives immediate interaction. This early burst of engagement signals to the algorithm that your post is valuable, pushing it higher up in the feed for broader reach.

Should I post more frequently or focus on the best times?

**It is better to focus on posting at the right times rather than simply increasing your overall volume.** While consistency is important, flooding feeds when your audience is offline leads to low engagement. Aligning a strategic, high-quality post with your audience’s peak browsing hours will always yield a higher ROI than posting blindly.
