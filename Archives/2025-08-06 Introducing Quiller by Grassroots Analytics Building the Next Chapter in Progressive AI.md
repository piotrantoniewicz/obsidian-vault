## 🎉 Quiller is joining Grassroots Analytics! 🎉

Dear Quillers,

I’m thrilled to share some exciting news with you ahead of our public announcement: Quiller is embarking on an exciting new chapter, one that will significantly expand how campaigns and nonprofits can use cutting-edge AI technology.

## Here’s what’s happening:

Quiller has been acquired by [**Grassroots Analytics**](https://blog.quiller.ai/e3t/Ctc/L1+113/d4HS-w04/VX4W-N3chN-mW4NS3Xf6N1d3rW7gFL9V5zRKVDN8fXWzY3lYM-W69sMD-6lZ3n-W3HHJQr3vqbdPW97tL565JvB7_W5S4GDL8dJ4NZN834PFmfs4D9W1WQ7YD4zHRK5W1NM_cz2mM8gGW55x-Cy91Rt0jW3BmJr67KYZtFW5vdWgQ61Xsj6W2_gy9v1nblkQW13SQRj2cHNNhW4VVdJT3sQzc5Vp97tn8hgRmkW6QSX973k5kgKW3p-Q9q50sZ-7W3NT3dr3LYCZ6W2L2M1d55c0NrW3JyY5B95XjD1W44CFGN2ctqr2W5V8QSQ63r5sQf5YWlRW04), a leader in political data and analytics. In addition to bringing even more resources, expertise, and support to our users, this acquisition is part of a bold new initiative from Grassroots Analytics to significantly expand what’s possible for generative AI in our space.

We launched Quiller two years ago to blaze a trail for responsible use of generative AI for campaigns and nonprofits. In our recent [Two Years of Quiller](https://blog.quiller.ai/e3t/Ctc/L1+113/d4HS-w04/VX4W-N3chN-mW4NS3Xf6N1d3rW7gFL9V5zRKVDN8fXWCq3lYM-W95jsWP6lZ3mlVK28485spVHrN72gg0BQrCr8W5jdwPP4Vy7-GV9TXwq6d9tM-W1W9yWN8MwCLHVdhldy23JqtHN8fZvzbwBtwbN1Qg6_F8fJjQVDr7Z33x_9BVW93CmPJ7dWW4zW5VT2Xw4h_fwVW7q13Ty7Vs9v-W5KK9Z111XQz8W7XFtBj71NXZfW9084NR87DgVgW1q2c5P6TLxDxN78zDg8kgHBPVGFjWp5Sn7D_W4nGcCg5Ggl1dW61hMCn7DZbxwW8dF4B624XLRRW36QW1T34YQ8jW5b-cvd1GLY0BW3srzLs5wW2MnW2yWqw54qmQ5WW6kf_Zf4wmtbKW4F2QqM5TfkltW3BmR_66-LC5JVb_3rp13SvscW7xSldp4vhnsWf7wj_Md04) milestone, I shared our hypothesis that *the next frontier for AI in our space will be the marriage of AI-tailored content generation superpowered by advanced data segmentation,* using next-level relevance to drive meaningful action for campaigns. Grassroots Analytics’ acquisition of Quiller opens the door to these transformative possibilities.

We have been continually impressed with the GA team every step of the way, and now we are so excited about their vision to take Quiller’s AI capabilities to the next level.

## What’s Changing and What Isn’t

**There is a lot to look forward to!**

- **🚀 Same tool, more support:** Users will be able to continue to enjoy using the Quiller platform exactly as they always have, with the added benefit of more resources to enhance user (and team) experiences in the tool. A core value at the heart of this acquisition is a commitment to ensuring that current and future Quiller users continue to have full access to Quiller and all of its significant features.
- **🔒 Same responsible AI, progressive values, & top-notch security leadership**: Quiller users will continue to have top-notch security, privacy, data ownership, and bias mitigation functions in the same top-quality, fine-tuned model that is fully designed for ethical AI use that empowers, not replaces teams. And Quiller will continue to be offered to Democratic and progressive organizations only.
- **👋 Same technical leadership for Quiller:** We are also delighted to share that Quiller CTO Marshall Seid will be joining the GA teamto continue overseeing the platform and integration you know and trust.
![](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:640/format:webp/1*xvLxoVhQaiGE_ycU18INpA.png)

Quiller CTO Marshall Seid presents at the Higher Ground Labs 2024 Tech Debrief in Phoenix, Arizona

- **🦋 Unwavering commitment to building the progressive tech advantage:** Expect new product features and innovations designed make it easier to create customized AI-driven content for campaigns and nonprofits. We have a shared vision for how generative AI plus data segmentation can provide even more powerful, satisfying experiences for donors and volunteers in the future, and are excited to begin exploring how to make this dream an empowering reality.

More than ever, I believe that despite all our concerns about Big Tech, **engaging with AI is the best way to secure outcomes that matter most for our communities**.

Additionally, launching the first genAI tool in the space gave us the opportunity to help work with partners to envision how we might measure success when it came to AI’s impact in campaigns.

Since then, ***you*** have helped us turn that vision into reality.
