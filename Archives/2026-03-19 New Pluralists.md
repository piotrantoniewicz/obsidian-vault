## Bringing a Movement to Life Online

New Pluralists is an ambitious and deeply necessary initiative—one that embraces the best of America while countering its most divisive forces. With a call for a multi-racial, multi-faith, intergenerational democracy, New Pluralists is shaping a vision for pluralism that is both urgent and evolving.

Yet, as the organization worked to refine its strategic focus, its digital presence was lagging behind. The existing website fell short of reflecting the depth and energy of its work, nor did it effectively engage funders and allies in the movement by showing the impact they have created. The challenge was clear: **how to create an online presence that inspires, educates, and mobilizes support while remaining adaptable to New Pluralists' evolving strategy—while also clarifying and contextualizing the broad concept of pluralism in a way that makes its value tangible to the public.**

## Tectonica’s Approach: A Strategic "Bridge" Website

Understanding the need for an immediate but flexible solution, Tectonica embarked on working on a “band-aid” website—one that would **clearly communicate New Pluralists’ vision in its current phase while allowing for future evolution.** Our approach focused on:

- **A compelling narrative** that makes pluralism accessible and demonstrates its impact.
- **A fresh, strategic web design** that works within the existing brand while adapting and augmenting elements that were lacking—integrating images, video, and page design to enhance clarity and engagement.
- **Brand Refresh:** The original brand concept referenced the connecting experience of music as a visual metaphor for pluralism, represented through a scientific sound wave. While conceptually powerful, this lacked the human-centered element at the core of pluralism in practice. Our approach retained the strengths of this metaphor while infusing a more tangible and relatable representation of pluralism's real-world impact.
- **Multiple Design Concepts:** We presented three design concepts to review, ensuring a divergence of approach, beyond what could be talked through, and allowing for the organization’s leadership to determine what best aligned with their vision and requirements.
- **A user journey that resonates with funders and stakeholders**, ensuring they see themselves as key protagonists in the movement.
- **A foundation for autonomy**, empowering New Pluralists’ team to manage and evolve their digital presence.

## Delivering a Digital Face That Matches the Mission

Tectonica’s work resulted in a sleek, narrative-driven website that provides clarity and momentum. By visually and structurally elevating New Pluralists’ brand, we created a space where funders, partners, and the public could easily grasp and engage with the organization’s purpose. The site is built not just for today’s needs but as a launchpad for the next phase—one that will fully integrate New Pluralists’ Theory of Change and long-term strategy.

- **Enhanced Resource Accessibility:** We have created a new Resources area that organizes and makes accessible a lot of material previously scattered across the site, including reports, case studies, and guides that give a comprehensive overview of the state of pluralism and the role of New Pluralists as an authoritative reference in the field.
- **Impact-Focused Landing Pages:** We have also created a set of landing pages to show the impact of the organization in numbers and the extension of their field partners and funding partners.
- **Modular Architecture for Future Growth:** Finally, the site has been built modularly using Jamstack technology, ensuring a more seamless integration of future components and sections with minimum disruption to both users and the admin team.

Tectonica’s work resulted in **a sleek, narrative-driven website that provides clarity and momentum**. By aligning digital strategy with organizational mission, we helped New Pluralists create a **meaningful and effective digital footprint** —one that fosters engagement, builds trust, and ultimately, strengthens the movement for pluralism.
