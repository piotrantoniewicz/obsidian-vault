#### So… What’s DCO?

Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO) is a programmatic advertising method that uses real-time data triggers to create personalized ads using dynamic elements (think: headlines, images, logos, colors). We can automatically mix and match these dynamic elements, while the platform uses real-time learning and adjusts to maximize conversions.

#### One campaign = a creative multiverse

We use DCO not to find the “best creative” but the creative that will perform best with that specific audience.

Audiences expect ad experiences tailored to their individual interests. With DCO, we can make hundreds or even thousands of creative variants without having to build every single ad from scratch. We can then further personalize these ads based on weather, location, and products, allowing us to meet audiences where they’re at (literally):

#### The Results

We’ve seen *incredible* performance results so far. After adopting DCO with one client, we saw:

- 20% more efficient CPAs on display
- 54% higher ROAS on CTV with dynamic wrappers

During the 2025 year-end giving season, several clients piloted DCO on display and CTV. Across the board, ads performed exceptionally well compared to previously top-performing standard display and CTV ads. Some specific client examples:

- DCO creative drove the most efficient cost per donation (CPD) and was the top driver of gift volume
- \>$1 immediate ROAS on display
- CTV ROAS was 6% above standard
