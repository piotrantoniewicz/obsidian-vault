## The Lead Magnet I Built

So, in the intro, I told you about the lead magnet that I built yesterday.

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/46d00d8b-e8a5-463c-9eea-0b515f2fd0a1/CleanShot_2025-10-23_at_13.18.04.gif?t=1761250863)

Here's what it does:

1. User lands on the page and clicks "Get Started"
2. Form collects their email, company type, role, and marketing goals
3. Gemini Pro analyzes their responses against the Moodboard prompt database
4. The tool then returns a recommendation for the most immediately useful prompt from all past editions of Moodboard + an explanation of why we chose it and how to use it
5. Everything (emails, company type, role, and goals) gets stored in Lovable's database for easy export to Beehiiv

## How to Build Your Own

Want to experiment for yourself?

I created a Lead Magnet meta-prompt that generates five simple, buildable concepts for your business 👇

**I need you to help me create 5 simple lead magnet concepts that can be built as web applications in Lovable. But first, I need you to interview me to understand my business.**

**Ask me 5 questions, one at a time, and wait for my response before moving to the next question.**

**Start by asking me about my business. Then, based on my answers, ask follow-up questions that will help you gather the context needed to create highly relevant lead magnet concepts for my specific situation.**

**After I answer all 5 questions, create 5 very simple lead magnet concepts based on my responses. For each concept, provide a complete prompt that includes the tool name, what it does, user inputs needed, and output provided.**

**Each lead magnet should:**

- **Be extremely simple to build and use**
- **Solve one specific problem for my target audience**
- **Be buildable with basic HTML/CSS/JavaScript in Lovable**
- **Include email capture functionality**
- **Provide immediate value in under 2 minutes**

**Format each of the 5 concepts as a complete prompt of 12 sentences or fewer that can be used to build the tool in Lovable. Start with phrases like "Build a…" or "Create a…" Focus on simplicity and immediate utility over complexity.**

**Let's start with question 1.**

Here’s how it works:

- Copy/paste the above meta-prompt into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini or your LLM of choice.
- It will interview you about your business (five questions, one at a time)
- After it collects enough information from the interview, it creates 5 Lovable prompts for tools like ROI calculators, assessment quizzes, comparison tools, or recommendation engines.
- Once you’ve chosen your concept, paste it into Lovable and watch it build your MVP.
