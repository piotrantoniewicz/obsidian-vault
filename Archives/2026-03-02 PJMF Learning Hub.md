### About This Hub

At PJMF, we believe that all civil society organizations will benefit if we can truly unlock deep knowledge sharing and skill building across the sector. The pace of AI and digital transformation is continuing to accelerate, and no organization should have to figure it all out on their own.

Through this Hub, we strive to make resources and learning experiences available to all nonprofits wherever they are in their journey towards becoming a data and AI-enabled organization. We are doing this in two ways:

1) vetting and curating what we believe are the strongest resources from across the web to guide your organization

2) creating hands-on learning opportunities to help provide concrete guidance and feedback to propel your organization on this journey

The Hub is organized by phases of what we refer to as the AI Journey. We hope you’ll take the time to understand where your organization is today, and that this organizing principle will help you identify what to tackle next.

You’ll notice opportunities to provide feedback throughout the Hub. Your input will be critical to help us make it as useful as possible to not only your organization, but others yet to come. We hope you’ll be an active participant in building something great!

#### Foundational Layers

There are also some ongoing areas of focus, which we call foundational layers. These areas may become more advanced as the journey progresses, however, they’re applicable across all phases.

AI Strategy: A strategy around how AI can be applied in a way that furthers the mission of an organization.

Stakeholder Engagement + Training: Opportunities to engage with the process of AI planning/adoption and deeper training to keep internal staff members and other key stakeholders included each step of the way.

Data Management + Governance: Management of data across the data lifecycle (collection, processing, storage, retention, deletion) and the policies, processes, roles, and responsibilities associated with that data.

Technology: The infrastructure, models, databases, tools, etc used to manage data and/or AI solutions.
