### Minnesota Public Radio

#### Experiment Summary

Timeframe: 02/13/2026 - 02/24/2026

To increase the donor conversion rate for visitors to the MPR News newsletter donation page, we tested adding a premium offer. The premium was available with a $5 monthly gift or a $60 one-time donation.

The test ran for just under 2 weeks, with traffic split evenly between the 2 versions.

#### Research Question

We believe that adding a premium for MPRNews Newsletter donation page visitors will increase overall donor conversion by connecting the impact of their gift with a meaningful thank-you—the Public Media Advocacy T-shirt. This pairing reinforces both the difference their support makes and the tangible appreciation they receive in return.

#### Flux Metrics Affected

The Flux Metrics analyze the three primary metrics that affect revenue (traffic, conversion rate, and average gift). This experiment produced the following results:

0% increase in traffic  
× 102.9% increase in conversion rate  
× 35.9% increase in average gift  
175.9% increase in revenue

#### Key Learnings

This experiment showed that adding a premium offer — a Public Media Advocacy T-shirt — to the MPR News newsletter donation page increased both conversions and revenue.

The version that included the premium generated a 34.3% lift in overall transactions, achieving 94% confidence. When we looked at gift type, the impact was even clearer: recurring donations increased by 102.9% (statistically significant), while one-time gifts rose by 11.8% (44% level of confidence) compared to the control.

The primary driver of growth was recurring giving. While the premium had a positive effect on overall donations, its strongest influence was on increasing the number of monthly commitments.

Based on these results, we should continue testing premium-based offers to grow conversion rates—particularly for recurring gifts. Pairing a clear mission-driven ask with a tangible incentive appears to motivate more visitors to take action. Future testing should explore different premium types to identify which offers resonate most and drive the greatest long-term donor value.
