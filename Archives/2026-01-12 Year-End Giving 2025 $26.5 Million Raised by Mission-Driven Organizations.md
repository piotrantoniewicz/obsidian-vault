## $26.5 Million Raised During Year-End Giving

Engaging Networks clients increased their donations by 11% year-over-year, totalling an astonishing **$26.5 million** raised on Dec. 31! Those dollars came in the form of 156,000 gifts, with an average amount of $170.

During the peak hours between 12 and 8 p.m. ET, our clients raised **$16.1 million**, accounting for **61% of the total dollars given**.

That kind of volume demands unwavering reliability from a fundraising and marketing automation platform. Engaging Networks was there during those high-pressure moments, ensuring that nonprofits can connect with donors, complete their outreach, and process transactions without any interruptions or hiccups.

“December 31st is one of the busiest days of the year on Engaging Networks,” said Leah Meers, Vice President of Client Success. “We’re committed to being there during high-volume giving windows, and we’re so grateful that we were able to support our clients and their supporters during year-end giving. Congratulations on a great year, and we can’t wait to see the amazing work you do in 2026!”
