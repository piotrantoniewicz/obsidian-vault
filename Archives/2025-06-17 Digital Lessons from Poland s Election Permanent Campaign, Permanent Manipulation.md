Poland’s latest election wasn’t just a political contest – it was a turning point in the evolution of digital campaigning. From AI-generated supporters to TikTok saturation and influencer candidates, the tools of disinformation, FIMI and manipulation have gone mainstream, reshaping how voters engage with politics.

If there is one takeaway from this campaign, it’s this: the line between real and fake no longer holds. What once would have been fringe or experimental is now mainstream.

[![](https://visegradinsight.eu/app/uploads/2025/05/Banners-18.jpg)](https://visegradinsight.eu/subscribe-to-visegrad-insight/)

This was the first Polish election where synthetic media, AI-generated content and disinformation were not just present – they defined the tone and reach of digital campaigning.
