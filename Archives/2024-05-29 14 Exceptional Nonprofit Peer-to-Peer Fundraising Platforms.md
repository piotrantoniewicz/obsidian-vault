## Peer-to-peer fundraising pricing considerations

Before we dive in, we want to clarify a few of the pricing notes you’ll see below. Our list references platform fees and processing fees. Keep these in mind as you review the different peer-to-peer fundraising platforms for your nonprofit:

- **A platform fee** is the price you will pay to use the peer-to-peer platform or fundraising tool.
- **A transaction or processing fee** is the price you pay each time your donors use a credit card to submit a donation to you or one of your fundraisers.

Don’t have the budget for an expensive peer-to-peer fundraising platform? There are plenty of [free and affordable software options](https://kindful.com/blog/free-nonprofit-software/) for your nonprofit to consider, too. However, keep in mind that free options may not offer the full range of features you need, or they may come with hidden fees that can add up over time.

## Considerations to make before choosing a peer-to-peer fundraising platform

How can you narrow down these options to find the right platform for your nonprofit’s needs? Walk through the following considerations to choose the platform that will serve your nonprofit most effectively:

**![Considerations to make before choosing peer-to-peer fundraising software (explained in the list below) ](https://kindful.com/wp-content/uploads/peer-to-peer-fundraising-platforms_considerations.png)**

- **Features.** Does the platform include all must-have features, including team and individual fundraising capabilities, a simple campaign page editor, social media and email sharing, gamification, and reporting tools?
- **User-friendliness.** Is the tool easy to use, even for staff members or supporters with less technical experience? Are the platform and campaign page editor easy to use from multiple devices, including mobile phones?
- **Demos.** Does the provider offer a demo or free trial? During the demo, did the tool seem intuitive and user-friendly?
- **Price.** What is your budget? Which tools align with your budget while providing the features you need to truly be successful? For platforms with processing fees, use your estimated campaign earnings to calculate predicted costs.
- **Support.** Does the software provider offer email or live chat support? Do they include templates to help set you on the right path?
- **Integrations.** Does the software integrate with your other fundraising tools, such as your CRM? Does it offer partnership integrations or will you need to use a third-party integration platform, like [Zapier](https://zapier.com/)?
- **Security measures.** What level of security does the peer-to-peer fundraising solution offer? Seek measures like data encryption, PCI compliance, and multi-factor authentication.
- **Customer reviews.** Search your top platform options on trusted third-party review sites like [G2](https://www.g2.com/) and [Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/). Are the tools positively rated by nonprofits similar to yours?

Use this checklist to ensure you’re covering all your bases to find a tool that will work for your organization over the long term.
