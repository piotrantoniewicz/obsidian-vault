## How To Create Your First Superniche Product

Your first digital product (say, an eBook) does not need to be a "masterpiece."

In fact, your digital product might only be 10 or 20 pages. It's not how long the digital product is, or how many words you've written, that determines its value. All that matters is that your digital product answers a valuable question to your target reader/customer.

The person buying your product isn't buying "a bunch of words" or even "a PDF."

What they're buying is *an answer to a super specific question that when answered solves 1 problem and 1 problem only.*

### The Black Friday Superniche Product Launch Plan

In this guide you will find a 10-Day plan that you can execute before Black Friday.

You can easily create your first digital product in a week—especially if you’ve been following the [Lean Writing approach](https://writewithai.substack.com/p/lean-writer-prompt-pack-how-to-turn). Ideally you will start 12 days prior to Black Friday, to give yourself enough time to promote your product before the “big shopping day.” Day 1 is Monday.

Here’s everything we are going to cover:

- **Day 1 - Monday:** Find a Superniche product idea and outline it
- **Day 2 - Tuesday:** Create Your Superniche product framework
- **Day 3 - Wednesday:** Prep The Page for your Superniche product
- **Day 4 - Thursday:** Design the product (graphics, layout, etc.)
- **Day 5 - Friday:** Create the landing page and set up the payment system
- **Day 6 - Saturday:** Run a small beta test of the product
- **Day 7 - Sunday:** Make revisions based on beta feedback
- **Day 8 - Monday:** Launch a viral giveaway/contest to build your email list
- **Day 9 - Tuesday:** Black Friday promotion and make your first $1!
- **Day 10 - Wednesday:** Put your marketing on auto-pilot
