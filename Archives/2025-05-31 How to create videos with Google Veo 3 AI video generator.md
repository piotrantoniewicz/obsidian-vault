How do you create a video quickly with limited funds, time and technical skills? Try [Google's Veo 3 AI powered video generator](https://gemini.google/overview/video-generation/) which costs $20/month. Here's how we used it to create a 20 second video in less than an hour to advocate for veterans' rights.

Veo3 converts written phrases into video automatically in a few minutes. We started with this prompt:

***"Disabled veteran in a wheel chair looks at the camera and says with disgust "I served my country. Lost my legs. Now they want to take away my veterans benefits to give tax cuts to billionaires! What happened to the America I fought for?" Have a patriotic soundtrack in the background. The veteran is pushing his wheelchair along in a hospital corridor with other veterans standing and sitting in wheelchairs."***

VEO 3 generated this video by creating the image of the veteran, the backdrop and the veterans in the background on its own. Even the veteran speaking and pushing his wheelchair forward was generated automatically.

### Refine your video

The first video created by Veo 3 was good but needed some edits which generated a new version of the video.

- The veteran should stay seated at the end of the video.
- The veterans in the background should be wounded with bloody bandages looking haggard. Make the hospital look dingy.
- He speaks slowly in a deep voice with disgust.
